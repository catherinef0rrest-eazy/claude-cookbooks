# GTM Fabric Brand Guidelines

This skill ensures all generated documents adhere to GTM Fabric brand standards for consistent, professional communication.

## Brand Identity

### Company: GTM Fabric
**Tagline**: "Unified Data. Expert Strategy."
**Industry**: GTM Intelligence & Data Strategy

## Visual Standards

### Theme Selection

GTM Fabric supports two theme variants to accommodate different use cases:

**Dark Theme** (Primary/Default):
- Digital presentations and web applications
- Client-facing ICP decks
- Modern, premium aesthetic
- High visual impact

**Light Theme** (Alternative):
- Print documents and PDFs
- Reports requiring readability in print
- Environments with bright ambient lighting
- Email attachments (better compatibility)

### Color Palette - Dark Theme (Primary)

**Background Colors**:
- **Deep Purple Black**: #06040f (RGB: 6, 4, 15) - Main background
- **Card Purple**: #120a1f (RGB: 18, 10, 31) - Card backgrounds, elevated surfaces
- **Sidebar Purple**: #0e0817 (RGB: 14, 8, 23) - Sidebar backgrounds

**Text Colors**:
- **Primary Text**: #f2f2f2 (RGB: 242, 242, 242) - Main content (95% white)
- **Secondary Text**: #a6a6a6 (RGB: 166, 166, 166) - Supporting content (65% white)
- **White**: #FFFFFF (RGB: 255, 255, 255) - Emphasis text

**Accent & Borders**:
- **Accent Purple**: #c084fc (RGB: 192, 132, 252) - Headers, highlights, CTAs
- **Default Border**: #3a2d4f (RGB: 58, 45, 79) - Standard borders
- **Card Border**: #261b35 (RGB: 38, 27, 53) - Card edges

### Color Palette - Light Theme (Alternative)

**Background Colors**:
- **White**: #FFFFFF (RGB: 255, 255, 255) - Main background
- **Light Gray**: #F8F9FA (RGB: 248, 249, 250) - Card backgrounds, alternating rows
- **Pale Purple**: #FAF8FC (RGB: 250, 248, 252) - Subtle tinted backgrounds

**Text Colors**:
- **Primary Text**: #1a1a1a (RGB: 26, 26, 26) - Main content (90% black)
- **Secondary Text**: #6C757D (RGB: 108, 117, 125) - Supporting content (50% black)
- **Dark Gray**: #343A40 (RGB: 52, 58, 64) - Strong emphasis

**Accent & Borders**:
- **Accent Purple**: #8B5CF6 (RGB: 139, 92, 246) - Headers, highlights (darker purple for contrast)
- **Border Gray**: #DEE2E6 (RGB: 222, 226, 230) - Standard borders
- **Light Border**: #E9ECEF (RGB: 233, 236, 239) - Subtle borders

**Semantic Colors** (Both Themes):
- **Success**: #28A745 (RGB: 40, 167, 69) - Positive indicators
- **Warning**: #FFC107 (RGB: 255, 193, 7) - Caution indicators
- **Error**: #DC3545 (RGB: 220, 53, 69) - Error indicators

### Typography

**Primary Font Family**: Arial, sans-serif

**Font Hierarchy**:
- **H1 (Titles)**: 30pt, Bold
- **H2 (Section Headers)**: 24pt, Semibold
- **H3 (Subsections)**: 16pt, Medium
- **Body**: 14pt, Regular
- **Small/Caption**: 12pt, Regular

**Font Weights**:
- Regular (400) - Body text
- Medium (500) - Emphasized text
- Semibold (600) - Headers, important UI

**Typography Colors by Theme**:

*Dark Theme*:
- H1: White (#FFFFFF) or Accent Purple (#c084fc)
- H2: Accent Purple (#c084fc)
- H3: Primary Text (#f2f2f2)
- Body: Primary Text (#f2f2f2)
- Caption: Secondary Text (#a6a6a6)

*Light Theme*:
- H1: Primary Text (#1a1a1a) or Accent Purple (#8B5CF6)
- H2: Accent Purple (#8B5CF6)
- H3: Primary Text (#1a1a1a)
- Body: Primary Text (#1a1a1a)
- Caption: Secondary Text (#6C757D)

### Logo Usage

**Logo Files**:
- `gtmfabric_logo_white.png` - For **Dark Theme** (dark backgrounds)
- `gtmfabric_logo_black.png` - For **Light Theme** (light backgrounds)

**Theme-Specific Usage**:
- **Dark Theme**: Always use white logo (`gtmfabric_logo_white.png`)
- **Light Theme**: Always use black logo (`gtmfabric_logo_black.png`)

**Placement Rules**:
- **PowerPoint**: Top-right corner on all slides
- **PDF Documents**: Top-left in header
- **Reports**: Top-left on first page, smaller in subsequent page headers
- **Size**: 0.8-1.0 inches width (maintain aspect ratio)
- **Clear space**: Minimum 0.25 inches padding on all sides
- **Never**: Distort, rotate, apply effects, or change colors

## Document Standards

### PowerPoint Presentations

**Slide Templates - Dark Theme** (Primary/Default):

1. **Title Slide**:
   - Background: Deep Purple Black (#06040f)
   - Title: 30pt, White (#FFFFFF), Bold, centered
   - Subtitle: 24pt, Accent Purple (#c084fc)
   - Date: 14pt, Secondary Text (#a6a6a6), bottom-left
   - Logo: Top-right, white version, 0.8 inches

2. **Section Divider Slide**:
   - Background: Deep Purple Black (#06040f)
   - Section title: 36pt, Accent Purple (#c084fc), centered
   - Logo: Top-right, white version

3. **Content Slide**:
   - Background: Deep Purple Black (#06040f)
   - Title bar: Accent Purple (#c084fc), 24pt, top of slide
   - Content area: Card Purple background (#120a1f) with rounded corners
   - Body text: Primary Text (#f2f2f2), 14pt
   - Page number: Bottom-right, Secondary Text (#a6a6a6)

4. **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) Slide**:
   - Background: Deep Purple Black (#06040f)
   - ICP Badge: Pill-shaped, Accent Purple (#c084fc) background with 30% opacity
   - Section headers: Accent Purple (#c084fc), 16pt, Semibold
   - Content area: Card Purple (#120a1f) background
   - Body text: Primary Text (#f2f2f2), 14pt
   - Vendor format: Bold with → arrow notation (e.g., "**Salesforce** → **Sales Cloud**")

**Slide Templates - Light Theme** (Alternative):

1. **Title Slide**:
   - Background: White (#FFFFFF)
   - Title: 30pt, Primary Text (#1a1a1a), Bold, centered
   - Subtitle: 24pt, Accent Purple (#8B5CF6)
   - Date: 14pt, Secondary Text (#6C757D), bottom-left
   - Accent bar: Thin line in Accent Purple (#8B5CF6) above or below title
   - Logo: Top-right, black version, 0.8 inches

2. **Section Divider Slide**:
   - Background: Pale Purple (#FAF8FC) or White (#FFFFFF)
   - Section title: 36pt, Accent Purple (#8B5CF6), centered
   - Accent element: Horizontal line or subtle gradient
   - Logo: Top-right, black version

3. **Content Slide**:
   - Background: White (#FFFFFF)
   - Title bar: Accent Purple (#8B5CF6), 24pt, top of slide
   - Content area: Light Gray background (#F8F9FA) with rounded corners
   - Body text: Primary Text (#1a1a1a), 14pt
   - Borders: Border Gray (#DEE2E6), 1pt
   - Page number: Bottom-right, Secondary Text (#6C757D)

4. **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) Slide**:
   - Background: White (#FFFFFF)
   - ICP Badge: Pill-shaped, Accent Purple (#8B5CF6) background or border
   - Section headers: Accent Purple (#8B5CF6), 16pt, Semibold
   - Content area: Light Gray (#F8F9FA) background
   - Body text: Primary Text (#1a1a1a), 14pt
   - Borders: Border Gray (#DEE2E6) for card edges
   - Vendor format: Bold with → arrow notation (e.g., "**Salesforce** → **Sales Cloud**")

**ICP Slide Structure** (Both Themes):

Required sections in order:
1. **ICP Badge/Title**: "ICP #1: [Descriptive Title]"
2. **Industries**: Comma-separated list
3. **Departments**: Comma-separated or bullet list
4. **Key Roles**: Job titles (CISO, VP IT, etc.)
5. **Technographic Fit**:
   - **Displacement/Modernization Signals**: Pain + products to replace
   - **Expansion Signals**: Readiness + products they currently use

**Layout Rules** (Both Themes):
- Margins: 0.5 inches on all sides
- Title position: Top 15% of slide
- Content area: Card-style backgrounds with rounded corners (2-4pt radius)
- Bullet indentation: 0.25 inches per level
- Maximum 5-6 bullet points per slide
- Use generous white space (minimum 0.25 inches between sections)

### PDF Documents

**Theme Selection for PDFs**:
- **Light Theme** (Recommended): Print documents, reports, attachments
- **Dark Theme** (Optional): Digital-only PDFs, presentations

**Page Layout** (Both Themes):
- Margins: 1 inch all sides
- Line spacing: 1.15
- Paragraph spacing: 12pt after

**Light Theme PDF** (Recommended):
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Logo: Black version in header (left side)
- Main headings: Accent Purple (#8B5CF6), 18pt, bold
- Subheadings: Primary Text (#1a1a1a), 16pt, semibold
- Body text: Primary Text (#1a1a1a), 11pt, regular
- Tables: Light Gray (#F8F9FA) alternating rows, Border Gray (#DEE2E6) borders
- Headers: Accent Purple (#8B5CF6) background, White text

**Dark Theme PDF** (Digital-only):
- Background: Deep Purple Black (#06040f)
- Logo: White version in header (left side)
- Main headings: Accent Purple (#c084fc), 18pt, bold
- Subheadings: Primary Text (#f2f2f2), 16pt, semibold
- Body text: Primary Text (#f2f2f2), 11pt, regular
- Tables: Card Purple (#120a1f) alternating rows, Card Border (#261b35) borders
- Headers: Accent Purple (#c084fc) background, White text

**Header Configuration** (Both Themes):
- Logo: Left side (theme-appropriate version)
- Document title: Center (theme-appropriate color)
- Page number: Right side
- Height: 0.75 inches

**Footer Configuration** (Both Themes):
- Copyright: "© 2025 GTM Fabric. All rights reserved." (left)
- Date: Center
- Classification: "Confidential" or "Internal Use" (right)
- Height: 0.5 inches
- Text color: Secondary Text (theme-appropriate)

### Excel Spreadsheets

**Theme Selection for Excel**:
- **Light Theme** (Recommended): Most use cases, better printing, universal compatibility
- **Dark Theme** (Optional): Dashboard-style spreadsheets, digital-only reports

**Light Theme Excel** (Recommended):
- **Headers**: Row 1, Bold, White text on Accent Purple (#8B5CF6) background
- **Subheaders**: Bold, Primary Text (#1a1a1a)
- **Data cells**: Regular, Primary Text (#1a1a1a)
- **Borders**: Thin, Border Gray (#DEE2E6), 0.5-1pt
- **Alternating rows**: Light Gray (#F8F9FA) background for readability
- **Charts**: Accent Purple (#8B5CF6) primary, coordinated secondary colors

**Dark Theme Excel** (Optional):
- **Headers**: Row 1, Bold, White text on Accent Purple (#c084fc) background
- **Subheaders**: Bold, Primary Text (#f2f2f2)
- **Data cells**: Regular, Primary Text (#f2f2f2)
- **Cell backgrounds**: Deep Purple Black (#06040f) or Card Purple (#120a1f)
- **Borders**: Thin, Card Border (#261b35), 0.5-1pt
- **Alternating rows**: Card Purple (#120a1f) background for readability
- **Charts**: Accent Purple (#c084fc) primary, coordinated secondary colors

**Chart Standards** (Both Themes):
- Primary series: Accent Purple (theme-appropriate)
- Secondary series: Success (#28A745), Warning (#FFC107), or lighter/darker purple variations
- Gridlines: Theme-appropriate border color, 0.5pt
- No 3D effects or gradients
- Clean, modern, flat design
- Accessible color contrasts

## Content Guidelines

### Tone of Voice

- **Professional**: Enterprise B2B tone, formal but approachable
- **Technical**: Data-driven, precise, analytical
- **Clear**: Avoid jargon, use simple language when possible
- **Strategic**: Focus on insights and actionable intelligence

### Standard Phrases

**Opening Statements**:
- "GTM Fabric provides..."
- "Our data intelligence platform..."
- "Unified data strategy for..."

**Closing Statements**:
- "Contact GTM Fabric for data-driven GTM intelligence."
- "Transform your GTM strategy with unified data."

### Data Presentation

**Numbers**:
- Use comma separators for thousands: 1,000
- Currency: $X,XXX.XX format
- Percentages: XX.X% (one decimal)
- Dates: Month DD, YYYY (e.g., January 15, 2025)

**Tables**:
- Headers in Accent Purple
- Alternating row colors for readability
- Right-align numbers
- Left-align text

**Vendor & Product Formatting**:
- Bold all vendor/product names
- Use arrow notation: Vendor → Product
- Examples:
  - Salesforce → Sales Cloud
  - Microsoft → Azure
  - Oracle → NetSuite

## ICP Presentation Specifics

### ICP Slide Structure

**Required Sections**:

1. **ICP Badge/Title**
   - Pill-shaped badge with ICP number and title
   - Background: Accent Purple with 30% opacity
   - Text: White, 16pt, Semibold

2. **Industries**
   - Header: "Industries" (Accent Purple, 16pt)
   - Format: Comma-separated list
   - Example: "Financial Services, Healthcare, Manufacturing"

3. **Departments**
   - Header: "Departments" (Accent Purple, 16pt)
   - Format: Comma-separated or bullet list
   - Example: "IT Security, Infrastructure, Compliance"

4. **Key Roles**
   - Header: "Key Roles" (Accent Purple, 16pt)
   - Format: Bullet list or comma-separated
   - Include job titles: CISO, VP IT, CTO, etc.

5. **Technographic Fit**
   - Main header: "Technographic Fit" (Accent Purple, 18pt)

   **5a. Displacement/Modernization Signals**
   - Subheader: "Displacement Signals" or "Modernization Signals"
   - Pain explanation: Detaileddescription
   - Vendor products: Bold with arrows
   - Example:
     ```
     Pain: Legacy endpoint security causing compliance risks
     Replacing: McAfee → ePO, Symantec → Endpoint Protection
     ```

   **5b. Expansion Signals**
   - Subheader: "Expansion Signals"
   - Readiness explanation: Brief description
   - Vendor products: Bold with arrows
   - Example:
     ```
     Readiness: Recent cloud migration
     Using: AWS, Microsoft → Azure, Okta
     ```

### ICP Content Best Practices

- **Keep vendor lists concise**: 2-4 products per signal
- **Use specific product names**: "Salesforce → Sales Cloud" not just "Salesforce"
- **Bold all vendor/product names** for scannability
- **Use arrow notation** consistently: Vendor → Product
- **Provide context**: Brief pain/readiness explanations
- **Maintain hierarchy**: Clear visual distinction between sections

## Quality Standards

### Before Finalizing

Always ensure:
1. Logo is properly placed and sized (white version on dark backgrounds)
2. All colors match brand palette exactly (#06040f, #c084fc, #f2f2f2)
3. Fonts are Arial (or Calibri fallback)
4. No typos or grammatical errors
5. Vendor/product names are bold with arrow notation
6. Professional dark theme maintained throughout
7. Adequate contrast for readability (minimum 7:1 for body text)

### Prohibited Elements

Never use:
- Bright, saturated colors that clash with dark theme
- Light backgrounds (except in specific PDF use cases)
- Decorative or script fonts (Comic Sans, Papyrus, etc.)
- 3D effects, shadows, or gradients (unless subtle)
- Competitor branding or unauthorized references
- Pixelated or stretched logos

## Special Instructions for ICP Presentations

When generating ICP presentations:

1. **Title Slide**:
   - Client name in title
   - "Ideal Customer Profiles" as part of title
   - Campaign type in subtitle
   - Date and GTM Fabric logo

2. **ICP Slides** (one per ICP):
   - ICP badge with number and descriptive title
   - All five required sections (Industries, Departments, Roles, Displacement, Expansion)
   - Card-based layout with clear visual hierarchy
   - Bold vendor/product names throughout
   - Use → arrows for all product associations

3. **Consistent Formatting**:
   - Same layout structure for each ICP
   - Parallel content organization
   - Equal visual weight across slides
